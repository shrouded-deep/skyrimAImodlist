## Description

MO2 reports 264 full-weight ESP/ESM plugins — 10 over the 254 hard cap. The list
will not launch correctly above 254. Need to bring the count to 253 or below
(leaving at least 1 slot headroom).

**Target:** ≤ 253 full-weight plugins.
**Approach:** ESL-flag small patch plugins that qualify (fewer than 2048 new FormIDs,
no records that other plugins reference by load-order-dependent index). Use
`housecarl_compact_plugin` to compact FormIDs and set the ESL flag. Do NOT merge
plugins — ESL-flagging is reversible and keeps the mod structure clean.

Candidates to evaluate first (small single-purpose patches are safest):
- Single-record patches authored in this session (e.g. Fork - City Stack JKs
  Interior Patch.esp — currently 1 record, trivially ESL-safe)
- Small compatibility patches with few or no new FormIDs
- Avoid ESL-flagging plugins that define many new records (spells, items, NPCs)
  or that other plugins forward by load-order index

Use `housecarl_cross_plugin_query` or `housecarl_read_record` to verify a plugin's
new FormID count before flagging. `housecarl_compact_plugin` handles both the
FormID compaction and the ESL flag in one step.

Work through candidates until the full-weight count is ≤ 253. Record every plugin
ESL-flagged and the final count in the Result.
