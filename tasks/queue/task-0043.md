## Pre-install research required (before any installs)

**Ryn's city outskirts/limits overlap with JK's Outskirts.**

Task-0041 only cataloged Ryn's Whiterun City Limits as a city-scoped mod, but
Ryn's likely has city limits or outskirts mods for other major holds (Windhelm,
Riften, Markarth, Solitude). JK's Outskirts already covers all four. Installing
both families on the same city without a patch is a conflict.

Before installing anything, Cursor must:

1. Search Nexus for all Ryn's city limits / city outskirts mods (search "Ryn's
   Windhelm", "Ryn's Riften", "Ryn's Markarth", "Ryn's Solitude" etc.).
2. For each Ryn's city mod found, check whether a JK × Ryn patch exists in the
   relevant patch hubs (78920, 87964, 91642, 95750, 73778, 128196).
3. Present findings to user (flag in Result before proceeding with installs) so
   a per-city JK vs Ryn decision can be made — or confirm a patch exists and
   both can coexist.

**This research step is a blocker. Do not begin installs until findings are
recorded and the user has confirmed which mods to include per conflicting city.**
