## Description

`decisions.md` (2026-07-12) names **Vokriinator Black** as the long-term gameplay
overhaul target for `Keizaal - Fork`. Research what installing it on top of Keizaal
vanilla+ would require: conflicts, patches, perk-tree interactions with Constellations
and other Keizaal gameplay mods, and whether SimonRim-disabled content affects the path.

**Nolvus reference implementation:** Nolvus Awakening V6 ships a full Vokriinator Black
implementation. Before building from scratch, evaluate whether its plugin list and patch
selections can be borrowed — the Nolvus instance is at `E:\Nolvus\Nolvus\` (do not
modify it). Specifically: identify which Vokriinator Black plugins and patches Nolvus
uses, whether those patches are bespoke Nolvus-only ESPs or standard community patches
available on Nexus, and what the delta would be to adapt that selection to Keizaal's
different mod base. This may significantly reduce research effort if the patch web is
mostly standard community mods.
