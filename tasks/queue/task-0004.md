## Description

The baseline scan (task-0002) only produced aggregate counts for the ~20
largest conflict categories, without individually auditing them. NPC_ and
Dialogue are the two highest-risk categories left unchecked — NPC record
conflicts commonly cause missing faces, wrong stats, or duplicate NPCs,
and Dialogue conflicts can silently break quest lines. Both are worth a
full line-by-line audit before the larger, lower-risk categories (Cell,
Armor, Weapon, etc.) get their turn in later tasks.
