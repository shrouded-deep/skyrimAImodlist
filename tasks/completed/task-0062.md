## Description

Compare the Anvil modlist against the Keizaal - Fork modlist and identify mods present in
Anvil but absent from Keizaal that could be worth adding to our fork. This is pure research
— no MO2 edits, no file copies. Output is an annotated candidate list for human review.

**Sources:**

- Anvil modlist: `D:\Skyrim AI Modlist\Anvil\profiles\Anvil\modlist.txt`
- Keizaal Fork modlist: `E:\Skyrim\profiles\Keizaal - Fork\modlist.txt`

## Process

1. Extract the enabled mod names from both modlists (lines starting with `+`; strip the `+`).
2. Identify mods in Anvil that are NOT present in Keizaal (by name, case-insensitive).
3. Filter out:
   - Separators (names ending in `_separator`)
   - Mods already in our additions (VB stack, city stack — see `tasks/completed/task-0050.md`,
     `task-0051.md`)
   - Mods already tracked as disabled/dropped in `modlist/decisions.md`
   - Anything Spaghetti-related (dropped, task-0055)
4. For each remaining candidate, look up the mod on Nexus (use housecarl_nexus_search or
   the mod page URL pattern) and note:
   - What it does (one line)
   - Whether it fits the **power fantasy lens** (expand capability/variety/spectacle; flag
     anything that adds survival friction, carry penalties, or difficulty-through-fragility)
   - Whether it has obvious compatibility concerns with VB stack / Keizaal's SimonRim base
   - ESL-flagged or full ESP (plugin budget note)
5. Group candidates by category (gameplay, content/quests, cities/locations, visuals,
   utilities/tools) for easier human triage.

## Curation lens (from AGENTS.md)

> Power fantasy build. Prefer mods that expand player capability, variety, and spectacle.
> Deprioritise or remove mods that impose survival friction, resource scarcity, carry
> penalties, or difficulty-through-fragility.

## Deliverable

Write findings to `modlist/anvil-keizaal-gap-analysis-2026-07-12.md`. Format:

```
## <Category>

| Mod name | What it does | Lens fit | Compat notes | ESL? |
|----------|-------------|----------|--------------|------|
| ...      | ...         | ✓ / ✗ / ? | ...         | yes/no |
```

Flag any mod that is an obvious must-have or obvious reject at the top of its section.
Do not make enablement decisions — just research and annotate. Human reviews and approves
any inclusions.
