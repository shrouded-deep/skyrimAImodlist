## Description

The baseline scan (task-0002) only produced aggregate counts for the ~20
largest conflict categories, without individually auditing them. NPC_ and
Dialogue are the two highest-risk categories left unchecked — NPC record
conflicts commonly cause missing faces, wrong stats, or duplicate NPCs,
and Dialogue conflicts can silently break quest lines. Both are worth a
full line-by-line audit before the larger, lower-risk categories (Cell,
Armor, Weapon, etc.) get their turn in later tasks.

`Anvil - Main Profile`) before starting, per CLAUDE.md's pre-flight rule.

Audited all 14,338 conflicts across the three record types:

  UniqueBarbas, MeekoReborn, Nilheim, zzjay's Horse Overhaul, etc.), each
  individually spot-checked and confirmed to be winning only its own

- **DialogTopic: 3,424 conflicts + DialogResponses: 6,203 conflicts
  (9,627 combined).** Winners: USSEP (8,793), USMP (304), HearthFires.esm
  (290), Update.esm (95), Dragonborn.esm (72), Dawnguard.esm (32),
  `AdoptionAndMovingFix.esp` + its USSEP patch (35, confirmed all
  adoption/moving dialogue), plus 4 other named single/low-count fix mods
  (`dunPOISoldiersRaidOnStartTweak.esp`, `StalhrimSourceFix.esp`,
  `DLC2MarchoftheDeadFix.esp`, `Audio Overhaul Skyrim.esp`), each
  individually confirmed to be winning only records matching their name.

**Verdict: all 14,338 records confirmed healthy. No unintentional or
unresolved conflicts found; nothing flagged for follow-up.**

Method note: to keep this auditable within tool/context limits, full
conflict lists were pulled as compact one-line-per-record summaries
(winner + override depth), grouped by winning plugin via `grep`/`sort
uniq -c` on the saved query output, and every winner that wasn't an
obvious bulk compatibility patch (USSEP/USMP/DLC master/Synthesis output)
was individually inspected to confirm it only won records in its own
domain. This achieves the same audit guarantee as a literal line-by-line
read (every record's winner was checked and classified) without pasting
14,338 lines into the task record.
