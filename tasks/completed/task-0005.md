## Description

Continuing the baseline scan follow-up (task-0002): Cell records were only
aggregate-counted, not individually audited. Cell conflicts affect
worldspace/interior placement, navmesh, and lighting — commonly one of the
largest categories by volume, so this is likely the biggest single audit
in the remaining set. Kept as its own task rather than grouped, since it
may be large enough to warrant checkpointing on its own.

## Result

Confirmed instance/profile matched before starting. Audited all 13,588
Cell conflicts using the same method as task-0004 (compact winner
summaries grouped via `grep`/`sort | uniq -c`, then individually inspected
every winner that wasn't an obvious bulk/generated-tool source).

**13,587 of 13,588 confirmed healthy.** Winners: `Occlusion.esp` (10,695,
generated), `ANV_SynWorldPatcher.esp` (1,518, generated),
`Lux.esp` + 6 named Lux compatibility patches (990 combined),
`ANV_SynW4ENBPatcher.esp` (189, generated), `DynDOLOD.esm`/`.esp` (95,
generated), USSEP (13), DLC/master overrides (Dawnguard/Dragonborn/
Update/CC — 53 combined), and 12 named single/small-count fix or content
mods (`IcyFixes.esm`, `Aspens Ablaze.esp`, `Navigator-NavFixes.esl`,
`Lightened Skyrim - merged.esp`, `Landscape and Water Fixes.esp`,
`Landscape Fixes For Grass Mods.esp`, `DwemerGatesNoRelock.esl`,
`shalidor's maze fixes.esp`, `man_malacathShrine.esp`,
`PraedysElderScrollSoulCairnFix.esp`, `Spaghetti's Faction Halls -
AIO.esp`, `Water for ENB (Shades of Skyrim).esp`,
`ANV_Lux - Volkihar Soundscape Overhaul - W4ENB.esp`) — spot-checked
several via `conflict_tree` (IcyFixes.esm, Lightened Skyrim - merged.esp,
Spaghetti's Faction Halls - AIO.esp) and confirmed standard
exterior-cell/region-data edits, no destructive pattern.

**1 flagged — needs attention:** `McmRecorder.esp` wins cell
`0BBCB2:Skyrim.esm` (`WEMerchantChests`) over `Update.esm`. Full diff
inspection shows this is not a routine override: it drops the cell's
navmesh from 1 entry to 0, and replaces the vanilla persistent placed
reference with the mod's own debug `MiscItem`
(`000D65:McmRecorder.esp`/`McmRecorder_MessageText`). This looks like an
accidental/dirty CK edit in a debug/MCM-recording utility mod, not an
intentional patch. Not fixed here (audit-only task) — see follow-up.
