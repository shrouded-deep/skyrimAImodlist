## Context

The city stack swap (task-0069) replaced JK's Skyrim AIO with our modular city
exteriors. The next layer is individual JK's exterior location mods (inns, shops,
etc.) and our Ryn's locations stack, both carried from Anvil.

Lost Legacy already ships two Ryn's entries:
- A **custom Ryn's Locations bundle** — a single mod folder containing many Ryn's
  ESPs (Lost Legacy's own packaging).
- **Ryn's Standing Stones Patch Collection** — a separate entry.

Both must be **disabled** in favour of our Anvil-sourced Ryn's stack. Watch for
the case where Anvil also carries "Ryn's Standing Stones Patch Collection" — if the
mod folder name is identical, the copy will silently collide with the Lost Legacy
entry. In that case: rename or merge rather than overwrite.

**Source list:** `D:\Skyrim AI Modlist\Anvil\` (MO2 instance)
**Target profile:** `Lost Legacy - Fork` at `D:\Skyrim\profiles\Lost Legacy - Fork\`
**Target mods folder:** `D:\Skyrim\mods\`
**Always close MO2 before editing modlist.txt or plugins.txt.**

---

## Step 1 — Identify JK's exterior mods to import from Anvil

In `D:\Skyrim AI Modlist\Anvil\mods\`, list all mod folders whose names begin with
`JK's` or `JKs` that are **not** AIO/Reduced Cut variants (those are already handled
by the city stack). Typical candidates: `JK's Riverfall Cottage`, `JK's The Bards
College`, `JK's Arnleif and Sons Trading Company`, etc.

Cross-reference against `D:\Skyrim\mods\` — skip any already present in the Fork.

Record the final import list before copying anything.

---

## Step 2 — Identify Ryn's mods to import from Anvil

In `D:\Skyrim AI Modlist\Anvil\mods\`, list all mod folders whose names begin with
`Ryn's`. These are the individual location mods (Ryn's Bleak Falls Barrow, Ryn's
Dragon Mounds, etc.) plus any patch collections.

Note which, if any, share a folder name with an existing `D:\Skyrim\mods\` entry —
flag these as collision risks before copying.

---

## Step 3 — Disable Lost Legacy's Ryn's entries

In `D:\Skyrim\profiles\Lost Legacy - Fork\modlist.txt`:

- Find the custom Ryn's Locations bundle entry (single mod folder, many ESPs inside)
  and prefix it with `-` to disable it.
- Find the `Ryn's Standing Stones Patch Collection` entry and prefix it with `-`.

Disable the corresponding plugins in `plugins.txt` for all ESPs that were active
inside those two mods (change `*Plugin.esp` → `Plugin.esp` for each).

---

## Step 4 — Copy mod folders from Anvil

For each mod identified in Steps 1 and 2 that is not already present in
`D:\Skyrim\mods\`:

- Copy the mod folder from `D:\Skyrim AI Modlist\Anvil\mods\<ModName>\` to
  `D:\Skyrim\mods\<ModName>\`

For any collision (same folder name exists in the Fork): **do not overwrite**.
Stop and record the collision in the Result — human will decide whether to merge
or rename.

---

## Step 5 — Add entries to modlist.txt

Using the separator rules from AGENTS.md:

- JK's exterior mods → place ABOVE `-JKs Skyrim_separator`
- Ryn's mods → place ABOVE `-Ryn's Mods_separator` (and BELOW `-City Stack_separator`)

Add each new mod as an enabled (`+`) entry in the correct position. Do NOT append
to the end of the file.

---

## Step 6 — Enable plugins

In `plugins.txt`, enable the plugins from all newly added mods (`*Plugin.esp`
format). Do not enable any plugin whose master is not present and active.

---

## Step 7 — MAST scan

Run missing-masters scan. Target: **0 missing masters**. Record plugin count
before and after.
