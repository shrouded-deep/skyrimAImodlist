## Description

Task-0013 updated Community Shaders to 1.7.3 but did not do a full audit
of what that version change actually broke or absorbed. Two real gaps
have already surfaced after the fact:

1. **EVLaS.dll incompatibility** — CS 1.7.x outright blocks EVLaS and
   disables all its own hooks/features when EVLaS is present. This was
   discovered live, at game launch, not during task-0013. EVLaS has since
   been disabled. Sky Sync (its replacement) is now core to CS as of
   1.7.0+ and should just need enabling in the CS in-game menu — confirm
   this rather than assuming.
2. **At least 5 other CS submods were also folded into core with 1.7.x**
   (beyond Sky Sync), and separately, **WetnessEffect needs a version
   bump to 3.1.0** specifically to stay compatible with the new CS
   version. Neither was caught by task-0013's pass.

This task exists because task-0013's CS submod check was too shallow —
it needs a real, complete pass across the entire CS-adjacent mod
ecosystem in this list, not just the handful of submods explicitly
labeled as CS plugins in MO2.
