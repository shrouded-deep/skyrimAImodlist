### Real conflict: Windhelm — WindhelmCandlehearthHall

**Record:** `016789:Skyrim.esm` (CELL: WindhelmCandlehearthHall)

| Plugin | Flags | Nav | Load order position |
|--------|-------|-----|---------------------|
| JK's Candlehearth Hall.esp | IsInteriorCell, **HasWater**, PublicArea | 1 navmesh | earlier (loses) |
| WindhelmSSE.esp | IsInteriorCell, PublicArea | 0 | later, depth=25 **(wins)** |

WindhelmSSE.esp's CELL header drops JK's `HasWater` flag, disabling the water in
Candlehearth Hall's well/pool (the water geometry JK's places in the cell requires the cell
flag to function).

**Patch authored:** `Fork - City Stack JKs Interior Patch.esp`
- Mod folder: `houseCARL - Fork - City Stack JKs Interior Patch` (at `D:\Skyrim\mods\`)
- Created via `housecarl_create_plugin` then `housecarl_forward_record` from
  `JK's Candlehearth Hall.esp` into the new patch
- Forwarded record: `016789:Skyrim.esm` with `Flags = IsInteriorCell, HasWater, PublicArea`
- Masters: Skyrim.esm, Update.esm (no dependency on JK's or WindhelmSSE in the header —
  record origin is Skyrim.esm; patch body only references vanilla masters)
- Plugin added to `plugins.txt` immediately after `WindhelmSSE.esp` (line 1451)
- Mod added to `modlist.txt` above `-JKs Skyrim_separator`
